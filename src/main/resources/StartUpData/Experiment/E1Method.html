Cell culture method

Cell line XYZ was harvested from a T:75cm2 flask into a final volume of 10 ml of media (protocol linked in the Methods section above).

A cell count was performed using the BioRad TC-10 cell counter and cells resuspended to a final volume of 12 ml at a concentration of 1e5 cells/ml in standard media containing 10% FCS.

100 µl of this suspension was then plated into each well of a 96-well plate using a multichannel pipette.

Plates of cells were incubated at 37ºC overnight with 5% CO2.
