XXX lab's ChIP protocol.

Used the 07/02/09 fem-2 extracts that were prepared under light fixation conditions (0.5 % formaldeyde for 4 minutes).

Used Protein G Dynal Beads: 50 to pre-clear and 100 to pull down ChIPed DNA for each ChIP.

Blocked 300 ul bead slurry (resuspended in 600 ul FA buffer) with 60 ug salmon sperm DNA and 60 ug BSA.

Used blocked beads to pre-clear extract.

REC-8 ChIP:

10 ul affinity purified SDI goat anti-REC-8 Q0802 antibody (5 ug total).

SYP-1 ChIP:

10 ul affinity purified goat anti-SYP-1 antibody (7.9 ug total).

Purified ChIPed DNA with AmPure beads. Quantified with PicoGreen:

REC-8: 0.126 ug/ml (* ~30 ul --> 3.78 ng)

SYP-1: 0.052 ug/ml (* 40 ul --> 2.08 ng)

Input: 7.96 ug/ml (* 40 ul --> 318.4 ng)

Made a library using all of the ChIPed DNA and 80 ng Input DNA.

For adaper ligation step I used 1 ul of a 1:50 dilution.

Purified at all steps using AmPure XP beads, except last gel extraction step where I used Qiagen kit.

Amplified DNA by PCR before size-selection step.

Ran on 2% agarose gel. Cut out from >200-300 bp. Purified w/ Qiagen gel extraction kit, using 2 mini-elute columns for each sample. Eluted in 15 ul warm buffer EB total (eluted 1 column, then w/ flow-through I eluted the other column).
