bam mutant female and male cDNA made on: 5/16/13   from RNA made on: 5/15/13

tubes #: 1-4

I am extracting RNA from male and female bam mutant Drosophila gonads, reverse transcribing to cDNA, and perfoming pcr on plus and minus reverse transcriptase samples of the resultant set of cDNA using primers that amplify products across exon-exon boundaries. In this way I can identify sex specific transcripts and therefore sex-specific splicing.
