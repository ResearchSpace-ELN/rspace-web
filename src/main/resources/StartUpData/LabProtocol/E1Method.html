1. Turn on centrifuge to 4 C and water bath for biorupter

  2. Make buffers

    • 100 ml PBS + protease inhibitors (1 tablet per 50 ml)

    • To 25 ml of this add 1 % formaldehyde (0.81 ml of 37 % formaldehyde).

    • Keep PBS + formaldehyde @ RT, but cool rest of FA on ice.

  3. Mill 10 ml of fem-2 popcorn, 5 min total @ 25 Hz.

    • 10 ml popcorn in a 50 ml cup.

    • Mill 1 min, open, stir, cool, mill 2 min, open, stir, cool, mill 2 more min to finish.

    • Scoop out milled worms into cold 50 ml conical, keep cold until fix

  4. Fast fix with 5X volume PBS + 1% formaldehyde

    • Add 25 ml PBS + formaldehyde, mix immediately until worms dissolve

    • incubate for 10 min @ RT rocking

  5. Quench formaldehyde by adding glycine to final conc of 125mM

    • 2.7 ml 1.5 M glycine per 30 ml (1/12 the volume)

    • incubate for 5 min @ RT rocking

  6. Spin down pellet and wash 3X

    • Spin @ 5000 x g for 5 min @ 4 C in Beckman/coulter centrifuge using JA-14 rotor

    • Use 25 ml ice cold PBS + PI per wash

    • Make FA buffer + 0.5 % SDS for next step

  7. Resuspend pellet in sonication buffer

    • Resuspend each pellet in 4 ml FA buffer + 0.5 % SDS + HALT (protease and phosphatase inhibitors, dilute 100 X – 50 ul per 5 ml)

  8. Sonication

    • Sonicate in 15 ml conical tube. Have all 6 slots filled w/ either samples or balances

    • Make sure probes don’t touch the side of the tubes.

    • Sonicate 30 sec on, 1 min off, for 34 cycles in biorupter. Total time: 55 min. Total sonication time: 18 min.

  9. Spin down samples

    • Remove 200 ul for analysis, spin down with other samples.

    • Pool extract, divide up into 3 tubes (125 ul each), and spin down @ max speed for 15 min @ 4 C.

    • Pool supernatants and aliquot into cryotubes (7 X 500 ul aliquots)

    • Flash freeze in liquid nitrogen, store at – 80 C.

  10. Sample for analysis

    • Separate supernatant from pellet and resuspend each in 400 ul FA buffer total volume (add 400 ul to pellet and 200 ul to supernatant).

    • Add 1 ul proteinase K (50-100 ug/ml) and incubate 1-2 hrs @ 55 C.

    • Put samples @ 65 C and incubate overnight to reverse crosslink.

  11. Phenol Chloroform extraction and RNase treatment

    • Spin down phase lock gel tubes for 3 min @ 800 rpm

    • Add 400 ul phenol/chloroform to each sample (1:1 volume ratio).

    • Mix gently and spin @ 800 rmp for 3 min

    • Remove top aqueous layer (300 ul) into fresh tubes

    • Add 300 ul chloroform (1:1 ratio), mix, spin

    • Remove top aqueous layer (250 ul), add 25 ul 3 M NaOAc, mix, add 687.5 ul ice cold EtOH, mix

    • Incubate @ – 20 C for 30 min

    • Spin @ max speed for 15 min @ 4 C

    • Pour off supernatant, add .75 ml 75 % EtOH

    • Spin 5 more min @ 4 C

    • Carefully remove supernatant and dry pellet

    • Resuspend in 250 ul TE + 1 ul RNase A (10 mg/ml)

    • Incubate @ RT for 10 min

    • Repeat all steps for phenol chloroform extraction. (Add 250 ul phenol/choloroform, spin, remove 200 ul, add 200 ul phenol, spin.) After chloroform extraction remove 160 ul of aqueous layer, add 16 ul NaOAc and 440 ul ice cold EtOH.

    • After EtOH precipitation resuspend pellet in 15 ul of TE.

    • Run 2 and 10 ul of resuspended DNA (bring final volume up to 10 ul and add 1 ul loading dye) on an 0.8 % agarose gel (100 V for 45 minutes).
