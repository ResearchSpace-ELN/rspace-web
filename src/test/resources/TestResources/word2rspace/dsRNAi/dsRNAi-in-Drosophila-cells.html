MAR’S PROTOCOLS.

 

dsRNAi in Drosophila tissue culture cells.

 

  1. Preparation of template DNA (from R.Adams).

 

We use Ambion MEGAscript T7 kits (Cat. 1334).

You need 15ug ds RNA for each well of cells. We find that you need quite a lot of wells for each experiment, i.e., 5 time points at 0, 1, 2, 3, 4 days, 1 sample for blot and 1 for immunofluorescence at each point = 10 wells =150ug dsRNA per experiment.

 

1. Make primers to make a PCR product of about 700 bp from your cDNA (or exon of genomic DNA). We PCR up the 5' end.

Put a T7 RNA polymerase promoter site at the 5' end of each primer (5'TTAATACGACTCACTATAGGGAGA 3' is the minimum sequence needed for efficient transcription; ANA USES 5'GAATTAATACGACTCACTATAGGGAGA 3').

 

2. Do 10x PCRs, and purify so that final concentration is at least 100ug/ml. Its OK to use non proof-reading enzyme, whatever gives the best yield (we use Boehringer Taq or NEB VENT). To purify the PCR products we use MoBio UltraClean PCR Clean-up DNA Purification Kit (cat. 12500-100 from CAMBIO) and put 3 PCRs through each.

This is the template for the transcription reaction.

 

3. Now follow kit instructions exactly (basically add nucleotides, buffer, enzyme, DNA an H2O to 20 ul).

We do 10 reactions per RNA. (I have not tried doing large scale reactions, could contact Ambion to see if this would work).Each reaction will make 20-50 ug RNA -so from 10 reactions you get 200-500ug RNA.

 

4. Pool reactions and precipitate with LiCL. Wash 2X in 70% EtOH after precipitation. The pellet is huge but quite sloppy and transparent.

Then resuspend in 100ul nuclease free H20. Determine concentration. heat at 65oC for 30 min to denature 2ndary structure, then place tube in beaker containing about 200ml H20 at 65oC and  leave on bench to cool to RT - about 45 min. Then test 1ul on regular agarose gel, it should run like ds DNA and be a pretty clean band.

 

RNA concentration should be 2-3ug/ul but we've sometimes used it at a lower concentration and it still works OK. Store at -20 oC. for up to four months.

 


dsRNAi in Drosophila tissue culture cells.

 

II. dsRNAi experiment (adapted from Kaufman’s lab).

 

-Check the concentration of exponentially growing culture of S2/Dmel2 cells. We dilute the cells every 3-4 days to keep them growing. Cells entering log phase 4-5 x 106 diluted to 106. (2ml culture + 8 ml serum free medium)

 

 

  1. For Western blot analysis:

 

-          Plate cells into 6-well microplates in DES Serum-free Expression medium (INVITROGEN cat no Q510-01) with added glutamine. In each well put 1 ml of media containing 106 cells.

-          Add 15 micrograms of dsRNA

-          Mix very well by swirling the dish.

-          Incubate for 30’ to 1 hour RT.

-          Add 2ml of Schneider’s Drosophila medium (GibcoBRL cat 11720-034) + 10% FBS and put back in the incubator at 27oC.

-          At each time point collect the cells. Determine density using haemocytometer to ensure homogeneous loading of the protein gel.

-          Boil samples in Sample buffer for 3’, sonicate and load on gel.
